“I dont know if you have noticed, but there is a common theme embedded across your journey! Diversity! Chameleons who can change body colours, rainforests that house a variety of organisms, survival items that do not really go hand in hand, and us, planners who come from different cultural and academic backgrounds that have come together to work for the same goal!”

We find ourselves surrounded by diversities in so many different contexts, yet, we often struggle to fully embrace the differences. Racial injustice, gender inequality, healthcare disparities due to differences in socioeconomic status are all examples of what is happening in the society right now due to lack of inclusivity.

We hope you, the future leaders of the next chapter, have learned about what diversity looks like and gained insights into understanding its impact.
