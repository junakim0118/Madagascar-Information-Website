Your guide: Chameleon

You will be joined by our versatile jungle guide, Chameleon! Brimming with self-love, it requested us to dedicate a page to itself. Below contains cool facts about it and its selfies as well as pictures taken by its fans. Examine its characteristics! They carry important hints about the treasure you all are looking for so make careful observations about it!

  • One very common misconception about chameleon is that they change their body colour for camouflage.

  • But in fact, they change their colours to regulate body temp, express emotions, and communicate with others(courtship, competitions,etc.).

  • They have varied colours, but most assume skin colours of green, yellow, cream, or dark brown.

  • They have panoramic vision so they can focus on two different things at once.

  • The eyes are built optimal for detecting and regulating light, focusing on objects rapidly, and enlarging. images.

  • They have a unique tongue projection. Their tongue can be twice as long as their body.

  • They can capture prey very quickly. The highest peak acceleration could be 200 times greater than acceleration due to gravity.
